How to write a blog


This is how you write a blog on our website

    1. Go to Editor page

      You will find the option for the editor page in the navigation bar or on the menu after clicking on the hamburger lines ☰


      If you haven't logged in, you will be redirected to login first and after login, you will be redirected to the editor page.

    2. Upload a banner image for your blog

      Now click on the button indicated with arrow 1, to upload a banner image for your blog

      The preview for banner image may not look like you had in your mind, but I promise it'll look good in the blog.

    5. Add headings in your blog

      There might be a heading you want to write for a paragraph.

      To write a heading, start on a new line and initialize your heading with '/'. A single '/' means heading 1. Similarly, you can add headings from heading 1 to heading 6 by incrementing number of '/' initialising your heading. i.e. '//' for heading 2, '///' for heading 2 and so on.

      Also after writing the heading, start a new paragraph on the next line.

Few points to keep in mind while writing a blog


    • Try not to use symbols at the start of a new line as it may cause errors with JavaScript.
    • Start on a new line when using symbols for links, youtube videos, headings, and images. Initialize with the symbol, and close with the same symbol for youtube videos and links. And then continue writing your content from a new line (please remember to continue with a new line every time).
    • For now, use only one categoryfor a blog


Rules


You must follow some rules while using this website or writing a blog.

    1. Be respectful to others

      No death threats, sexism, hate speech, racism (NO N WORD, this includes soft N).

      No doxxing, swatting, witch hunting.

    2. No Advertising.

      We do not allow advertising here of any kind.

    3. No NSFW content.

      Anything involving R-rated content is not allowed.

      NSFW = Not Safe for Work.

    4. No spamming.

      Do not write spam blogs! You will be warned first by the deletion of your blog. For the second time, the user's account will be deleted.

    5. Do not write about sensitive topics

      This isn't a debating server, keep sensitive topics out of here.

    6. No malicious content

      No grabify links, viruses, crash videos, links to viruses, or token grabbers. These will result in account deletion.

    7. Banner, images, emoji Rules

      No NSFW allowed.

      No racism.

      No brightly flashing pictures to induce an epileptic attack.



Help and support

If you want some help with blogs, report a blog or delete a blog you wrote, message in the "report-delete" channel on the discord server.

Use the following format-


Blog- "put blog title here"

Action- delete/report/help

Reason- reason for deletion or reporting


We will soon look into your problem and take suitable action.
